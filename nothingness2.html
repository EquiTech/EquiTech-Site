Nothingness 2

Nothingness is back and better then ever! After a very succesful release of nothingness we started development on nothingness 2 which takes the game far beyond what you could imagine. We took the good from nothingness, added in improvements and kept building on the features everyone loved from the first game. Expect a more versitile arsenal of wepons allong with the addition of vehicals such as tanks, bikes, and planes! With the ability to parachute to your intended location to really surprise your enemies. New maps, New weapons, New characters, hidden bonuses inside each level. You never know what to expect now!
