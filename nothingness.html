Nothingness

The Award-Wining Nothingness is our first attempt at getting into the FPS market and has been succesful thus far. The games is a mixture between Counter strike and Modern Warfare where you get to play the game the way you enjoy it. With a mixture of objective maps such as capture the flag, bombing an area and getting out, and man of the mountain players can choose what they want! Add in free for all death matches and the ability to create and share your own map and the possibilities are endless! There is plenty of fun to be had from any gamer!
